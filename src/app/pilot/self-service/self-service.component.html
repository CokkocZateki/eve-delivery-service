How does this work?

The process is as follows:
process: 1. pilot sees list, his current volume and the expected total jita sell value 2. pilot picks an item 3. this sets him as the assignee 4. pilot pastes the items into eve 5. pilot checks the price 5a. if the price has change more than xx%, the pilot flags the order for review 5b. if the price is ok, the pilot buys the items 6. the pilot creates a courier to his jf alt 7. the pilot continues from 1 until he reaches his limit (volume or isk) 8. the pilot sends a consolidated confirmation mail
