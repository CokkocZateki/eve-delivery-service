Help - Fitting Ships

 Please fit this doctrine fot the client.
  1. The order's items should be in your cargo. Please right click the ship, navigate to "Multifit" and select the correct fitting as shown in the shipment box.
  2. If more than one sets of the selected fit are available, please adjust the "Ships to fit" to the maximum value (it should auto update to the maximum value when you click next to the number.
  3. Do NOT check "Fit Rigs?" as the rigs shall be placed in the ship'scargo.
  4. Now fit and contract it.
